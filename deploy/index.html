GETTING STARTED

Looking for some fun? Looking to get some excersice? Downingtown Rock Gym is the perfect place for you! Bring your friends and family with you for a fun day out! Or come yourself for a healthy workout. Our staff will assist both first time climbers and experienced climbers.

DOWNINGTOWN, PA – The Downingtown Rock Gym (formerly Vertical Extreme) is a community based indoor rock climbing facility. Conveniently located in Chester County, outside of Philadelphia. With over 10,000 square feet of indoor rock climbing, offering bouldering, top rope, auto belays and lead climbing.

Downingtown Rock Gym’s walls feature overhangs, boulders, aretes, chimneys, caves, and lead walls. Providing rock climbing experiences for all ages and abilities. Beginner rock climbing lessons, advanced rock climbing lessons, outdoor rock climbing, and lead climbing lessons are offered, with Group Rates available.

DAILY RATES

BOULDER PASS

$12.00

Bouldering is a form of free climbing performed on shorter walls without the requirements of ropes or a harness. Our Boulder Pass grants two hours of endless climbing on our boulder walls up to a height of 12 feet within our gym. Rental harness or shoes are not required.

DAY PASS

$14.00

In addition to access to all 12 foot bouldering walls, our Day Pass grants access to all top ropes and auto-belays. Our staff will perform a short orientation to familiarize all new climbers with gym operations and equipment to ensure safety for all. Prior knowledge of rope handling is required for belay certification. Harnesses are required and are available for rent.

10 PUNCH PASS

$115.00

Like the day pass, our 10 Punch Pass offers full access to the gym’s facilities. However, the 10 Punch Pass allows for 10 seperate visits to the gym. The pass may also be used to pay for someone else’s entrance into the gym.

BIRTHDAY PARTIES

LARGE GROUPS

If you are looking for a new and exciting way to celebrate a birthday, then the Downingtown Rock Gym has just the thing. Indoor rock climbing has quickly become a popular recreational activity for all ages and ability levels.

This birthday party is all rock climbing. Climbers will start with bouldering (low climbing) and progress to roped climbing. Climbing walls provide varying degrees of height and difficulty so there is plenty for all ability levels.

Included is a 2 hour party, harness rentals, unlimited bouldering (low climbing), unlimited roped climbing, private party room, and all paper products. We do the set-up and clean-up. You just handle the food! Downloadable invitations or E-vite.
